AUDIO FILE SLOTS (User-supplied — not bundled)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The theme system supports optional user-supplied audio files.
No audio files are bundled with Seanime. Drop your own files at:

  seanime-web/public/themes/naruto-opening.mp3    — Naruto background music
  seanime-web/public/themes/naruto-event.mp3      — Nine-Tails event audio clip
  seanime-web/public/themes/bleach-opening.mp3    — Bleach background music
  seanime-web/public/themes/bleach-bankai.mp3     — Bankai event audio clip
  seanime-web/public/themes/one-piece-opening.mp3 — One Piece background music
  seanime-web/public/themes/one-piece-gear5.mp3   — Gear 5 event audio clip

You are solely responsible for ensuring you have the right to use
any audio files you place in these slots. Seanime does not endorse
any copyright infringement.
